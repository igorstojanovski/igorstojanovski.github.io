Hello! I'm Igor, a passionate Java Developer based in Sweden. With many years of experience in the tech industry, I have honed my skills in Java, with a comprehensive focus on end-to-end service delivery. My experience encompasses all stages of the software development lifecycle, including not only development, but also testing, Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD), and monitoring.

I started this blog to share my knowledge and experiences with other aspiring developers. Here at Igorski.co, I explore advanced Java topics, software engineering best practices, and the latest in tech.

In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my family and reading a good sci-fi book whenever I get the time for it. I believe in continuous learning and sharing, which drives the content you see on this blog. I aim to post bi-monthly, covering a range of topics that intrigue me and hopefully, you too!
